You fill out a spreadsheet:
You type in the names of your topics and tell the script where the PDFs or study guides are saved on your computer.
The script rotates your Google accounts:
Google limits how many videos you can make per account. The script automatically divides your topics into groups of 4 and assigns them to your 10 different Google accounts.
It generates the study materials on NotebookLM:
The script logs into each account in the background.
It uploads your study materials and instructs NotebookLM to create:
An Explainer Video (an MP4 video overview).
A Slide Deck (a PowerPoint presentation).
A Practice Quiz and Flashcards (to test your knowledge).
It saves everything to your Google Drive:
The script downloads the generated files from NotebookLM.
It compresses them into a single backup .zip file.
It uploads the individual files and the .zip file to your Google Drive into a folder named after the topic.
It cleans up your laptop:
Once the upload to Google Drive is finished, the script permanently deletes the files from your computer so they don't take up any disk space.
It runs invisibly:
Everything runs quietly in the background without any browser windows popping up on your screen, so you can continue using your laptop normally.
